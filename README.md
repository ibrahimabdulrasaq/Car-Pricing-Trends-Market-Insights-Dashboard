## ğŸ” Key Insights

### 1. Automatic Transmission Consistently Commands a Price Premium

Automatic vehicles consistently record higher average prices than manual and semi-automatic vehicles.

**Insight:** Transmission type is a major price driver, with buyers willing to pay a premium for convenience.

### 2. Vehicle Prices Have Increased Over Time

The price trend shows a steady upward movement in recent years.

**Insight:** Model year strongly influences pricing, with newer vehicles maintaining higher resale value.

### 3. Premium Brands Maintain Higher Average Prices

Premium brands consistently record higher average prices compared to mass-market brands.

**Insight:** Brand reputation significantly impacts pricing beyond vehicle specifications.

### 4. Fuel Type Influences Pricing

Diesel and hybrid vehicles show higher average prices compared to petrol vehicles.

**Insight:** Fuel efficiency and long-term ownership considerations influence buyer willingness to pay.

### 5. Market Demand Favors Automatic Vehicles

Automatic vehicles dominate buyer preference across the dataset.

**Insight:** Customer demand aligns closely with higher pricing, reinforcing the value of automatic transmission vehicles.

* Highlight fuel efficiency (diesel & hybrid) in vehicle listings to justify pricing
